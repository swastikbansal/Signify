Signify

Indian Sign Language Translator

🎯 What is Signify?

Signify is a powerful browser extension that brings Indian Sign Language (ISL) translation directly to your YouTube viewing experience. Using advanced 3D animation technology, Signify transforms video content into visual sign language, making it accessible to the deaf and hard-of-hearing community.

✨ Key Features

🤖
3D Avatar Translation
Real-time 3D animated avatar that translates spoken content into Indian Sign Language with smooth, natural movements.
🎥
YouTube Integration
Seamlessly integrates with YouTube, automatically detecting videos and providing sign language translation overlay.
⚡
Auto-Start Feature
Automatically begins translation when a video starts playing, ensuring you never miss important content.
📱
Side Panel Support
View the sign language avatar in a dedicated side panel for better visibility and control.
🎨
Customizable Settings
Adjust avatar size, animation speed, and display preferences to match your viewing needs.
🌐
Favorite Sites
Quick access to your favorite websites with custom site management and favicon support.

🚀 How to Use Signify

Install the Extension
Add Signify to your browser and grant necessary permissions for YouTube access.
Navigate to YouTube
Go to YouTube.com and find any video you want to watch with sign language translation.
Activate Signify
Click the Signify button that appears on the video player, or use the extension popup to configure settings.
Choose Display Mode
Select between floating avatar overlay or side panel view based on your preference.
Enjoy Accessible Content
Watch as the 3D avatar translates the video content into Indian Sign Language in real-time.

⚙️ Settings Guide

Auto-start on video play
When enabled, Signify will automatically begin translation as soon as a YouTube video starts playing. This ensures seamless accessibility without manual intervention.
Show floating avatar
Controls whether the sign language avatar appears as a floating overlay on the video or in the dedicated side panel. Enable for overlay mode, disable for side panel only.

🛠️ Troubleshooting

Avatar not appearing?
• Ensure you're on a YouTube video page
• Check that the extension has necessary permissions
• Try refreshing the page

Side panel not working?
• Make sure your browser supports side panels
• Check if side panel is enabled in browser settings
• Try reloading the extension

Translation quality issues?
• Ensure stable internet connection
• Try adjusting video quality settings
• Report issues through our support channels

🎓 About Indian Sign Language

Indian Sign Language (ISL) is the primary sign language used by the deaf community in India. Signify aims to bridge the communication gap by providing accurate, culturally appropriate ISL translations for digital content. Our 3D avatar system uses motion capture technology and linguistic expertise to ensure authentic sign language representation.
